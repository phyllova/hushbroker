To become a client, you will have to complete a simple six-step application form.


If you require assistance with any part of the application form, please reach out to our friendly staff via email, phone or our live chat function. 


After you complete all the application form steps, your application will be sent to our operations team for review and approval. We hope to process all applications within 24 hours. 


You will not be able to create a live trading account until your application has been approved. However, you can fund your wallet in preparation for your application being approved. 


To learn more about the Secure Client Area functionality, please visit our other support articles to become familiar with the processes of depositing, withdrawal, account creation, platform downloading, and more.

As a regulated broker, we require a one Proof of Identity and one Proof of Address document to verify your account. Only after you verify your account can you trade on your live trading account.

 

Upload your photo ID

• We accept a Passport, Driver’s License, or National ID Card.
• Upload the front and back of your Driver’s License and National ID Card separately.

 

Upload your Proof of Address

• We accept a Bank Statement, Telephone Bill, Utility Bill, Insurance Policy, or Lease Agreement.

• Your name and address must be visible and dated within the last 3 months.
• The name/ logo of the issuer should also be clearly visible.

It is important to note that you must set a password for every MT4, MT5, TradingView, and ForexRover Trade account that you create. You will use this password when logging into the trading platform combined with your account number for MT4 and MT5, and combined with your email address for TradingView and ForexRover Trade. 


Bear in mind, if you forget your password in the future, you can repeat this process to reset your password at any time.


Login into the Secure Client Area



Click on the Accounts tab. 



Click on the menu dots on an account card and select “Rest password”.



Type your new password and click SUBMIT.

    To become a Partner, Affiliate, or an Introducing Broker (IB)with ForexRover Markets, visit the Partners tab. 

    Select APPLY/Begin Application to begin the process. After doing so, you will be taken to the Applications tab. 


    Find the PARTNER tab at the top of the page to display the partner’s application form. 


    As you will have filled in your personal details in the past, you will not have to resubmit these details. 

    Rather, all we require is that you fill in the requested PARTNER DETIALS and agree to the terms and conditions. 


    Click NEXT once you are ready to submit your application.


    Our team will then review your application and get back to you within 24 hours.

     

    After your application has been approved, you will be notified via email. 


    When you revisit the Partners tab, you will be presented with the partner types for which you are approved to apply. 


    Select Begin Application on the partner type in which you are interested.
